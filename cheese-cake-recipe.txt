Ingredients:
- 15 graham crackers, crushed
- 1.5 cups of white sugar 
- 2 tbsp butter, melted
- 4 8 oz. packages of cream cheese
- 3/4 cups of milk
- 1/4 cup of flour
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup sour cream
- 4 eggs

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter. 
   Press into the bottom of a spring form pan.
3. In a bowl, mix cream cheese with sugar until smooth. Blend
   in the milk and then the eggs, one at a time. Mix just enough
   to encorporate. Then mix in the sour cream, vanilla, and flour
   until smooth. Poor into spring form pan.
4. Bake in over for 1 hour. Turn off oven and let the cake sit in
   the oven with door closed for another 5 to 6 hours.
5. Remove cake from oven and chil in the refrigerator until served.
